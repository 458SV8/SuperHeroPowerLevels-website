SUPER HERO POWER LEVELS

About This Visualization

This visualization is inspired by classic superhero trading cards. It uses cubes to show power levels for attributes like Intelligence, Strength, Speed, Durability, and Power. The marks are cubes (showing power levels), text (for names and labels), and images (representing the superhero). The channels are length (number of filled cubes showing magnitude), color (blue for filled and grey for empty), and text styling (bold and size for emphasis). Design Category: This is a "novel" visualization because it uses a unique and interactive representation of superhero data.
